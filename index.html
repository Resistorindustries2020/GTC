Material

Walk the bustling streets of central Joburg’s shopping district and you will see Shweshwe and its riot of colour every where – the famous fabric is both a touch of home and heritage, and loved around the world.Also known in the Sesotho language as shoeshoe for King Moshoeshoe from Lesotho, the fabric is a great example of how a cloth, popularly worn in blue, brown and red, has been passed from one generation to another over centuries.We develop most of our products using this amazing eco friendly material.
